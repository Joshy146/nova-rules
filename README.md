## 📋 About This Project
This is a single-page, responsive HTML/CSS document designed to provide our players with a clean, easy-to-read, and mobile-friendly breakdown of our community expectations. 

It covers essential community structures, including:
* Eligibility & Management Authority
* Privacy & Conduct Expectations
* Prohibited Content & Cheating Policies
* General Gameplay & Roleplay Restrictions
* Priority Cooldown Mechanics

## 🛠️ How to Update the Rules
If you are a member of the Nova Roleplay Management team and need to update the rules:
1. Navigate to the `index.html` file in this repository.
2. Click the **pencil icon** (Edit this file) in the top right.
3. Update the text within the appropriate `<h2>` or `<div class="card">` sections.
4. Scroll to the bottom, write a short summary of what you changed, and click **Commit changes**.
5. The live website will automatically update within 1–2 minutes!
