Nova Roleplay

Community Standards & Server Rules

By accessing Nova Roleplay — including our FiveM server, Discord, or any affiliated platform — you agree to comply with the rules and policies outlined below. Failure to do so may result in disciplinary action, up to and including permanent removal.

1. Eligibility & Acceptance of Terms

  • You must be at least 13 years old to join our Discord, per Discord TOS.
  • By participating in Nova Roleplay, you confirm you have read and agreed to these rules.
  • Claiming ignorance of the rules does not exempt you from punishment.

2. Management Authority & Enforcement

Nova Roleplay Staff reserves the right to:

  • Remove users or restrict access at any time.
  • Issue punishment with or without warning if necessary.
  • Apply discipline based on context, intent, history, and common sense.
All staff decisions are final. Public arguments regarding moderation actions are prohibited.

3. Community Conduct Expectations

All members are expected to act respectfully and maturely. The following are strictly prohibited:

  • Harassment, bullying, or targeted behavior
  • Discrimination or hateful language of any kind
  • Excessive toxicity beyond reasonable roleplay
  • Personal attacks, stalking, or intimidation
  • Impersonation of users, staff, or real-world entities
Severe misconduct (doxxing, swatting, sexual harassment, network attacks) will result in immediate removal and may be reported to authorities.

4. Privacy & Personal Safety

The following actions regarding private data are strictly prohibited:

  • Sharing private or identifying information without consent
  • Posting real names, addresses, locations, or IDs
  • Sharing private or restricted social media profiles
  • Posting images or videos of someone’s face without permission
  • Recording or streaming from private spaces without consent

Even if information is publicly available, using it to harass or target someone is prohibited.

5. Prohibited Content

The posting or distribution of the following content is banned:

  • Sexual content, pornography, or nudity
  • Sexual violence or exploitation (zero tolerance)
  • Child exploitation (reported to authorities)
  • Terrorism, extremism, or mass-casualty threats
  • Graphic or excessive gore
  • Illegal activity or encouragement thereof
  • Malware, viruses, or malicious links
  • Spam or disruptive posting
  • Unauthorized advertising or solicitation
  • False or intentionally misleading information

6. Cheating, Exploits & Server Integrity

The following actions will result in immediate server bans:

  • Mod menus or injected cheats
  • Bug or mechanic abuse
  • AFK farming
  • Phishing or scams
  • Account sharing or selling
  • Fake distress reports or misinformation
  • Tampering with Nova Roleplay systems
Abuse of exploits must be reported immediately.

7. General Gameplay Requirements

  • Working microphone required (limited exceptions may apply)
  • English must be used in public interactions
  • Discord access is mandatory
  • Follow staff chain of command
  • Realistic and appropriate usernames and character names
  • No self-promotion of other servers or services

8. Roleplay Restrictions

The following roleplay elements are strictly forbidden:

  • RDM or VDM
  • Sexually explicit or disturbing character models
  • Hitman, vigilante, or contract-killing RP
  • Impersonating law enforcement or emergency services
  • Interfering with passive departments
  • Raids or kidnappings in greenzones
  • Scene interruption without RP justification
  • Priority creation during cooldowns
  • Intentional immersion disruption

9. Roleplay Mechanics & Definitions

  • No Metagaming
  • No FailRP
  • FearRP is mandatory
  • NLR applies after death
  • No combat logging
  • No OOC during active RP unless staff-directed
  • Third-party VOIP is IC communication

10. Zero-Tolerance Roleplay

Engaging in the following roleplay scenarios will result in a permanent, unappealable ban:

  • Sexual assault RP
  • Suicide RP
  • Terrorism or mass-casualty RP

11. Priority Cooldown Rules

What Is a Priority Scene?

  • Armed robberies
  • Shootouts
  • High-speed pursuits
  • Hostage situations
  • Large-scale criminal activity

During Cooldown, You May NOT:

  • Start new priority scenes
  • Escalate into violent situations
  • Bait law enforcement
  • Bypass cooldowns via loopholes

Allowed During Cooldown:

  • Civilian and business RP
  • Traffic stops
  • Investigative RP
  • Social and character development
Staff discretion applies at all times. Claiming ignorance of cooldowns is not a valid excuse.
